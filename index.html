About My Story

I've grown up loving technology ever since I was a kid - but not necessarily growing into my passion for development until I was in college.

As I entered college, I dove into the world of development doing everything that I could to learn; I took 20+ Linda.com courses on just about every language from Java to Ruby on Rails, I made multiple niche websites targeting Amazon affiliate links in an effort to earn passive income, and I took part in several start-ups as a front-end developer.

I continued learning as much as I could by myself, but made the mistake of spreading myself to thin rather than deeply understanding the fundamentals. I slowly stopped developing and went into operations/sales as the perfet developer stepped in to help me with my App and I discontinued my time at the start-ups to take on this role.

After around 1-2 years of sales and other business ventures, I realized how truly passionate I was for building software and seeing apps come to life... and I've always wanted to come back. After a lot of thought, I decided to enter the LearningFuze program to quickly jump-start my development skills and feel comfortable building full stack Apps in a real-world environment.

Now I am more confident than ever to leave a mark in the technology industry and add as much value as I can to my company and team.
